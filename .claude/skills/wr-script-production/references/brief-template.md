# Creative brief template (SK-B3)

One page, filled before drafting (skippable only when a ratified calendar row
IS the brief — social profile, Phase 5 §3.2):

- **Audience:** the specific person (segment ref into audience.md).
- **Problem:** what they believe / don't know that costs them.
- **Central lesson:** one sentence a distracted viewer could restate.
- **Hook direction:** mechanism preferences, if any.
- **Emotional movement:** from → to (e.g., anxious → oriented).
- **CTA:** the single ask.
- **Length/format:** platform + duration band.
- **Required facts:** F-ids / packet claim ids that must appear.
- **Prohibited territory:** task-specific additions on top of compliance.md
  (concept-level conflicts refuse at the brief, not after drafting).
