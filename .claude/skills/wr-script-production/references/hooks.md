# Hooks (SK-B3)

When asked for hooks, deliver **ten**, not three — they're cheap and the user
is picking one. Vary the mechanism (≥3 across the batch): question · number ·
contradiction · second-person accusation · quiet statement · specific-person
snapshot.

A hook is **one breath**, not a word count. "The people who keep it are
boring. That's the whole finding." is two sentences and works because the
second lands after a beat. Say it aloud; if you had to inhale in the middle,
it's two hooks.

Never: banned openings, manufactured urgency, a question the reader has never
once asked themselves.
