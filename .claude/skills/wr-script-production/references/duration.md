# Duration discipline (SK-B3)

**190 words ≈ 90 seconds** for to-camera financial content. Not 210: the
usual 140-wpm figure is podcast pace; a speaker pauses after a claim and the
viewer needs the pause to absorb it.

Bands (spoken words, markers excluded — `scripts/duration_check.py`):

| Format | Band |
|---|---|
| reel-90s | 170–200 |
| reel-60s | 110–135 |
| reel-30s | 55–70 |

Write, then read aloud against a timer. Under 80s it's thin; over 100s, **cut
a beat rather than speeding up**. If the overrun can only be fixed by
shrinking a required disclosure — escalate; the disclosure never shrinks.
