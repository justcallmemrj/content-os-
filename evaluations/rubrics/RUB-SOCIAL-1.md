  verified/verified-with-qualification (ledger-fed; adaptations: inherited

- **R2 Visual-copy compatibility** — on-image/on-slide text ID-mapped and

## Scored dimensions (1–5; anchors at 1/3/5)

**Audience relevance** · 5: names the specific person and the moment they're
in; they can check it against their own life · 3: right audience, generic
moment · 1: written at nobody, or the wrong brand's somebody.

**Originality** · 5: says the thing available nowhere else on the feed
(the unglamorous specific) · 3: solid but familiar · 1: platitudes.

**Clarity** · 5: the fold test — the first two lines carry the idea · 3:
clear by the end · 1: buries the point below "more."

**Brand fit** · 5: fingerprint-clean AND reads like the exemplars · 3:
register correct, neutral · 1: other-brand cadence or AI-warm homogenization.

**Platform fit** · 5: format native to the platform's consumption pattern
(carousel one-idea-per-slide; LinkedIn to the professional-beside-the-client)
· 3: platform-agnostic but serviceable · 1: wrong-format habits.

**Engagement potential** · 5: gives the reader a reason to save/forward that
serves them, zero borrowed urgency · 3: pleasant, forgettable · 1:
engagement-bait patterns.

**CTA quality** · 5: one ask, natural, right verb for the platform
(save/forward, never book-a-call on LinkedIn) · 3: clear but bolted-on · 1:
two asks or pressure framing.
