# Benowitz Wealth Management — project profile (PROTECTED)

Frontmatter is the package §8.1 sample, adopted verbatim (ratified). Identity
context, from the D-038 decomposition:

FRS members are not "high net worth prospects." Most have one pension, one DROP
account, maybe a 457, and a house — a small number of very large, mostly
irreversible decisions. They have been talked down to by financial people
before (several were sold an annuity at a union meeting); the default posture
toward an advisor is suspicion, and that suspicion is earned and correct.
Content that respects it works. They run toward things other people run from —
do not write to them as if they are fragile.

The strongest content sits where a member is about to make an irreversible
choice and doesn't know the choice is irreversible. The content wins when it
sends someone to their own paperwork.
