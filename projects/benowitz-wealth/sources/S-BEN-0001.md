# Florida Statutes §121.091

Primary statute for FRS benefit payment, including subsection (13), the
Deferred Retirement Option Program. Verified live at build seeding
(2026-07-10): confirms the 96-calendar-month maximum participation (with
limited K-12 instructional/administrative exceptions) and the
terminate-all-employment-relationships requirement with employer verification;
failure to terminate voids the DROP election.
