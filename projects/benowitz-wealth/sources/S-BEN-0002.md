# IRS — Rollovers of retirement plan and IRA distributions

Verified live at build seeding (2026-07-10). Confirms: direct
rollover/trustee-to-trustee transfer → "No taxes will be withheld from your
transfer amount"; and "A retirement plan distribution paid to you is subject to
mandatory withholding of 20%, even if you intend to roll it over later."
