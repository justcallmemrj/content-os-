# DEC-BEN-0002 — "fee-only" hold LIFTED (firm-wide)

## Caveat recorded (not a blocker)

The filed ADV never uses the literal phrase "fee-only" — the claim is
**substantively supported** (compensation is client advisory fees
exclusively; nothing in the filing contradicts it) but not **verbatim
anchored**. Recommended CCO follow-up, outside this system: add fee-only
phrasing at the next ADV amendment so marketing and filing use the same
words.

## Consequences

1. The 36-caption DUC back-catalog's "fee-only" disclosure wording is now
   permissible as-is.
2. ⚠ The DUCAT_BTS book manuscripts say "fee-based" — WRONG under this
   decision. They are vendored provenance copies (never edited in-repo);
   the correction belongs in Wes's manuscript workflow before any
   publication. Their own compliance memo already flags this.
3. BEN-C6 / DUC-C6 rules updated to point here; compliance.md bumped to
   1.1.0 in both projects.
4. Writers asked for "fee-only" no longer substitute-and-flag; the E4
   pattern DEC-BEN-0001 modeled is retired for this term.
