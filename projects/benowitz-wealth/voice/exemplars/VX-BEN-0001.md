**Instagram:** "DROP might be the most misunderstood FRS benefit — so here it
is in plain English. You reach normal retirement, 'retire' on paper, but keep
working. Your pension locks in and starts piling up in a trust fund instead of
going to you. When you finally leave, you take that balance as a lump sum or
roll it over. It's only for Pension Plan members, and the timing of when you
enter matters a lot. Worth learning before you're eligible."
