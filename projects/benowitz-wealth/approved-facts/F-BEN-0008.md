  The Social Security Fairness Act (signed January 5, 2025) repealed the
  Windfall Elimination Provision and the Government Pension Offset,
  retroactive to benefits payable for January 2024 onward; the repeal affects
  only benefits connected to pensions from employment not covered by Social
  Security — and most FRS-covered employment participates in Social Security,
  so many members with a pure FRS career were never subject to WEP/GPO.
