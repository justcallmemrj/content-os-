# Florida Statutes §1006.74

Florida's NIL statute. Verified live at tranche-3 seeding (2026-07-11):
confirms intercollegiate athletes "must have an equal opportunity to control
and profit from the commercial use of her or his name, image, or likeness"
and be "protected from unauthorized appropriation and commercial
exploitation"; also mandates at least two financial-literacy / life-skills /
entrepreneurship workshops (minimum 5 hours each) before graduation, which
"may not include any marketing, advertising, referral, or solicitation by
providers of financial products." History line shows repeated amendment
(ch. 2020-28; 2021-35; 2021-217; 2023-4) — direct evidence NIL rules change
frequently. Earlier drafts' agent/attorney and contract-disclosure provisions
no longer appear in the current text; do not cite them from memory.
