# IRS — Roth IRAs

Verified live at tranche-3 seeding (2026-07-11): "You cannot deduct
contributions to a Roth IRA" and "If you satisfy the requirements, qualified
distributions are tax-free"; contribution amounts depend on filing status and
income. NOTE (honest scope): this page does NOT itself state the earned-income
/ taxable-compensation requirement — that rule was verified on the IRS IRA
contribution-limits page (S-DUC-0010). Facts citing the compensation
requirement must cite both records.
