# IRS — Estimated taxes

Verified live at tranche-3 seeding (2026-07-11): "Taxes must be paid as you
earn or receive income during the year, either through withholding or
estimated tax payments"; individuals "generally have to make estimated tax
payments if they expect to owe tax of $1,000 or more"; the year is divided
into four payment periods; underpayment can trigger a penalty (safe harbors:
owe < $1,000 after withholding/credits, or paid >= 90% current-year / 100%
prior-year tax); Form 1040-ES is the individual computation vehicle.
`published` approximates the current page revision (IRS pages are updated in
place); thresholds and safe-harbor percentages are re-verified against the
live page before any post cites them.
