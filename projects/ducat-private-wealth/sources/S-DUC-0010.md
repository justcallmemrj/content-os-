# IRS — IRA contribution limits

Verified live at tranche-3 seeding (2026-07-11): combined traditional + Roth
IRA contributions for a year cannot exceed "your taxable compensation for the
year" when compensation is below the annual dollar limit — the rule behind
"you need earned income to contribute." Page also lists the annual dollar
limits (2026: $7,500 / $8,600 age 50+), which are volatile and must never be
copied into content from this record — route to the live page per the caption
set's own discipline. `stability: volatile` because the dollar figures change
annually.
