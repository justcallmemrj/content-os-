Visibility has a cost nobody mentions on signing day: visible money attracts claims. Lawsuits, "misunderstandings," opportunistic disputes — athletes see more of all three.

Asset protection is the discipline of arranging your life so one bad event can't reach everything. The basics:

Liability insurance, sized up — auto and home limits raised, plus an umbrella policy layered on top: broad, comparatively inexpensive coverage above your other policies.

Separation — business activity in its own entity, so a business problem stays a business problem.

Signature discipline — personal guarantees and casual cosigning hand your assets to other people's risks.

Structure gets built before trouble, never after. An attorney who does this work can map your version.

There's an uncomfortable fact behind athlete wealth: the more visible the money, the more claims find it. Accidents become lawsuits faster, disputes escalate quicker, and settlements get priced to the jersey, not the facts. Asset protection is the unglamorous practice of making sure one event can't reach everything you've built.

The foundation is insurance, deliberately oversized. Auto and homeowner liability limits raised well above defaults, then an umbrella liability policy stacked on top — broad coverage that begins where the underlying policies stop, and typically among the cheapest protection per dollar of coverage a visible person can buy.

The second layer is separation. Business ventures live in their own entities, with their own accounts and real formalities observed — so a failed venture or a business dispute claims business assets, not the family's. Titling and account structure matter too, and vary by state.

The third layer is behavioral: signatures. Personal guarantees, casual cosigning, and handshake authority over accounts are how carefully built walls get bypassed — voluntarily. Decline by policy, not by mood ("everything goes through my advisor").

What asset protection is not: hiding assets after trouble starts — that's too late and can be worse than useless. The structure is legitimate precisely because it's built early, in daylight, with a qualified attorney mapping the version your state and situation call for.
