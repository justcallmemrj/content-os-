# Ducat Private Wealth — project profile (PROTECTED)

From the D-038 decomposition. Three distinct audience stages, and content
usually has to pick one: **pre-money** (college athletes with NIL deals,
artists with a first advance — income arrived before infrastructure did),
**peak** (under contract, earning at a level that will not repeat, surrounded
by people with opinions), **after** (the earning window closed — sometimes
at 27). What all three share: **the career is front-loaded and the life
isn't.** Everything Ducat says is downstream of that sentence.

They are not financially illiterate; they are financially *unadvised* — which
is different, and they know the difference.
