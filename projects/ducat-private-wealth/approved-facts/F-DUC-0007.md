statement: >-
  Florida law (Fla. Stat. §1006.74) gives intercollegiate athletes an equal
  opportunity to control and profit from the commercial use of their name,
  image, or likeness and protection from unauthorized appropriation; it also
  requires institutions to provide at least two financial-literacy, life
  skills, and entrepreneurship workshops (minimum 5 hours each) before an
  athlete graduates, and those workshops may not include marketing,
  advertising, referral, or solicitation by providers of financial products.
