claim_key: ira.roth.requires-taxable-compensation
statement: >-
  Roth IRA contributions are made with after-tax money (contributions are not
  deductible) and qualified distributions are generally tax-free; a year's
  total IRA contributions cannot exceed the contributor's taxable compensation
  for that year — so contributing requires earned income, which NIL
  compensation generally provides.
