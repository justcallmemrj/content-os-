statement: >-
  US federal income tax is pay-as-you-go: tax must be paid as income is earned
  or received, through withholding or estimated tax payments. Income that
  arrives with no withholding — most NIL and other self-employment income —
  generally requires quarterly estimated payments (four payment periods,
  Form 1040-ES; generally triggered when expecting to owe $1,000 or more), and
  underpaying during the year can add a penalty on top of the tax.
