# Ducat compliance rules (PROTECTED)

**The firm-wide envelope applies in full:**
@projects/_shared/ria-compliance-envelope.md

## Brand-specific rules

- **DUC-C1 — Non-affiliation on every piece.** Ducat Private Wealth is not
  affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any professional league, team,
  players' association, university, athletic conference, or NIL collective.
  Athletes will assume otherwise if unsaid. Verbatim texts: `disclosures.md`.
- **DUC-C2 — No real named public figures** as examples, positive or negative
  (firm-wide rule, but this brand trips it constantly — restated here).
- **DUC-C3 — No broke-athlete cautionary tales** as a persuasion device.
- **DUC-C4 — Never read as recruiting a minor.** NIL content aimed at high
  school athletes speaks to the situation, not the individual.
- **DUC-C5 — Tax content carries the tax referral disclosure** (DISC-DUC-TAX-01);
  jock-tax and multi-state content uses softened constructions ("may owe,"
  "the rules are different") — rules vary by state and contract structure.
- **DUC-C6 — "fee-only" PERMITTED** per `projects/benowitz-wealth/decisions/DEC-BEN-0002.md`
  (firm-wide lift, 2026-07-11, ADV-verified); "fiduciary" remains equally available.
