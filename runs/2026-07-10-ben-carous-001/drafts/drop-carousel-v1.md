**Slide 1:** Your DROP payout arrives as a single check. It comes with a decision attached. Most members never hear it called a decision.

**Slide 2:** Most members picture a payday. Half of that is right.

**Slide 3:** Your monthly pension turns on by itself.

**Slide 4:** The lump sum doesn't. It waits for your instruction.

**Slide 5:** Cash gets taxed as that year's ordinary income. Any payout sent to you directly has twenty percent withheld for federal tax.

**Slide 6:** A direct rollover is the other route. The money moves trustee to trustee into an IRA or another qualified plan, nothing withheld, still tax-deferred.

**Slide 7:** Choose cash, and it's taxed in the year it pays out. Choose the rollover, and the timing becomes yours.
