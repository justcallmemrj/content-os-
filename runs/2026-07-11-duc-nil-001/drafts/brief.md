# Creative brief — 2026-07-11-duc-nil-001 (SK-B3 brief template)

> work order: T-2026-0711-008 · project: ducat-private-wealth · state: brief
> packet manifest: packet-manifest.yaml · voice: brand-voice.md@1.0.0

- **Audience:** `audience.md#pre-money` — a college athlete with a first NIL
  deal, or the parent beside them. Income arrived before any infrastructure
  did: no HR, no withholding, an eighteen-year-old signing things. Nineteen
  with a first check, reading on a phone.

- **Problem:** they file the check under categories they already know —
  salary, scholarship money, a reward — and every one of those categories is
  wrong. Wrong category means missed contract terms, untouched tax share, and
  a compliance rule they never knew applied.

- **Central lesson (one sentence a distracted viewer could restate):**
  *NIL money is business income — a contract and a tax bill come attached,
  and nobody handles either for you.*

- **Hook direction:** quiet contradiction — name the miscategorization
  ("not a paycheck") without hype or fear. No urgency mechanics.

- **Emotional movement:** flattered-but-unmoored → oriented. The viewer should
  feel *seen rather than sold*: someone finally named what this money actually
  is and what it quietly expects of them.

- **CTA:** curriculum-locked style — one reflection question + save prompt.
  NO lead-gen, no link, no booking language (work order prohibition +
  curriculum north star: understanding before action).

- **Length/format:** instagram-reels · reel-90s · 170–200 spoken words
  (190 ≈ 90s per SK-B3 duration discipline).

- **Required facts:**
  - F-DUC-0001 — NIL = payment for commercial use of name/image/likeness;
    business income with contracts and tax obligations attached; not a salary
    from the school, not scholarship, not free money. (Definitional spine:
    BEAT ONE/TWO + TURN.)
  - F-DUC-0002 — rules vary by state, school, level; change frequently;
    school compliance office is the first checkpoint. (BEAT THREE; always
    paired with F-DUC-0001 per its usage notes.)
  - Supporting (approved memory, not work-order-pinned): F-DUC-0004 —
    no employer withholding on 1099-type income; recipient owes the tax.
    (BEAT THREE mechanism; softened per DUC-C5.)

- **Beat plan (~90s spoken):**
  1. `[0:00 HOOK]` — the first NIL check is not a paycheck; it only looks
     like one.
  2. `[0:05 STAKES]` — name the person: the athlete who just signed, the
     parent over their shoulder. Money arrived; instructions didn't.
  3. `[0:15 BEAT ONE]` (what they think is true) — it reads as salary /
     school money / a reward. None of those.
  4. `[0:35 BEAT TWO]` (what's actually true) — payment for the commercial
     use of who you are; business income; a contract underneath; tax
     generally not taken out first.
  5. `[0:55 BEAT THREE]` (what nobody told them) — no one withholds for you;
     part of the check may already be owed; rules differ by state, school,
     level and keep changing; compliance office first.
  6. `[1:15 TURN]` — reframe: you didn't receive free money, you started a
     small business; the check was its first sale.
  7. `[1:25 CTA]` — reflection: do you know exactly what you're selling?
     Save this for the next deal.

- **Register notes (brand-voice.md@1.0.0):** composed; fewer words, more
  weight; sentence median target 9, p90 max 18; never perform enthusiasm;
  banned openings ("In today's world", "As a financial advisor", "Let's talk
  about"); avoided phrases honored; **no sports metaphors applied to money**;
  name the unglamorous specific; one CTA only.

- **Prohibited territory (work order + compliance.md v1.1.0):** no lead-gen
  CTA; no school marks or real-athlete likenesses; no named public figures
  (DUC-C2); no broke-athlete cautionary tales (DUC-C3); never read as
  recruiting a minor — speak to the situation (DUC-C4); tax content softened
  ("may owe," "generally") + tax referral disclosure travels with the piece
  (DUC-C5); no dollar figures, rates, or year-specific rules (structural
  claims only); disclosure = persistent on-screen block, never spoken.

- **Disclosure routing:** video + space-constrained ⇒ DISC-DUC-SHORT-01
  verbatim in a persistent `[TEXT ON SCREEN:]` block; condition: full
  DISC-DUC-01 (+ DISC-DUC-TAX-01, since the script touches taxes) must appear
  in the profile/caption, and the short text states so ("See profile for full
  disclosures").
